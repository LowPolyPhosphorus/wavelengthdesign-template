How It Works

Our process (the real version)

Every project is different, but here's generally how we roll. No hidden fees, no surprise charges, no BS.

1. Discovery Call

Free 30-minute chat to see if we're a good fit. We talk about your goals, timeline, and budget. No pressure, just honest conversation.

2. Proposal

We send a detailed proposal with scope, timeline, and pricing. You take your time, ask questions, and we adjust until it feels right.

3. Kickoff

Once you sign, we schedule a kickoff meeting. This is where we dig deep into your business, audience, and vision.

4. Research & Strategy

We study your market, competitors, and audience. This groundwork ensures our design decisions are smart, not just pretty.

5. Design Concepts

We present initial concepts and walk you through our thinking. This is collaborative— your feedback shapes the direction.

6. Refinement

We take your feedback and refine. We're not precious about our work—we care about getting it right for you.

7. Final Delivery

You get all the files, assets, and documentation you need. We don't hold anything hostage—it's all yours.

8. Support

We stick around for questions and tweaks. Our job isn't done when the files are delivered—we want you to succeed.

FAQ

Questions we get asked

How much do you charge?

It depends on the project, but our branding projects typically start around $8,500, websites around $15,000, and UX/UI work around $12,000. We'll give you an exact quote after our discovery call.

How long does a project take?

Most branding projects take 4-6 weeks, websites 8-12 weeks, and UX/UI projects 6-10 weeks. Rush jobs are possible but cost extra.

Do you work with startups?

Absolutely! We love working with new businesses. We offer startup packages that are more budget-friendly but still include everything you need to launch strong.

What if I don't like the designs?

We include multiple rounds of revisions in every project. If you're not happy, we keep working until you are. We've never had a client walk away unsatisfied.

Do you offer monthly retainers?

Yes! We have ongoing relationships with several clients where we act as their design team. Monthly retainers start at $5,000/month.

Do you work remotely?

We're based in Portland but work with clients all over. Most of our process is virtual, though we're always happy to meet in person if you're local.
